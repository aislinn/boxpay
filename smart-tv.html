Smart TV

From the comfort of their couch, your customers can purchase items in your Smart TV applications with their mobile phone. After the consumer enters their mobile phone number into the TV with their remote or voice command, we send them a text message with a PIN for them to enter back into their TV. The charge then goes to their phone bill.
